Sections and how you use it;

    Arduino Power:

    To power your Arduino, you can use the battery, or connect the HDMI to your chromebook and insert the code.

    Servo-Motor;

    Once the Arduino is powered, you can use the servo-motor to rotate the light anywhere from 0 degrees, to 180 degrees.

    Button and Lights;

    When you press the button, you are able to turn on the LED's in the projector. There are three modes;

  • First press: The light shines at it's maximum out-put.
  • Second Press: The light becomes dimmer, and less of an outline.
  • Third Press: The LED's turn off completely, and the cycle restarts.
    • This part of the build is really important because it determines if the spotLIGHT works or not.

    Set the Setting!;

    Lastly, turn off the lights, and let the spotlight shine!
